1. General Provisions

At Tools Finance, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we process personal data in connection with our mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1. Registration and Authentication Data

  • Email address (for registration, login, and password recovery)
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)

2.2. User Data

  • User profile (email, subscription status)
  • Token balance
  • App usage history

2.3. Chat and Message Data

  • Chat history with the AI assistant
  • Sent messages and responses
  • Attached files (documents, images)
  • AI-generated documents

Important: Files attached by the user are not stored on our servers. They are transmitted to the AI for processing without being retained.

2.4. Subscription and Payment Data

  • Information about subscription purchases
  • Transaction details (via Apple/Google IAP)
  • Subscription status and expiration dates

2.5. Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device information (type, OS, app version)
  • App performance data

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1. Main Purposes

  • Providing AI assistant services
  • Processing requests and generating responses
  • Managing subscriptions and payments
  • Improving service quality
  • Technical support

3.2. File Processing

  • Analyzing attached documents (CSV, Excel, PDF)
  • Generating reports and tables
  • Temporary storage for processing

4. Third-Party Services

4.1. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the app. Google AdMob may collect:

  • IP address
  • Device advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

You can learn more about how Google uses this information on their Privacy & Terms page. You can also reset or disable ad personalization in your device settings.

4.2. Apple/Google In-App Purchases

For payment processing, we use:

  • Apple App Store (for iOS devices)
  • Google Play Store (for Android devices)

These platforms process payment data according to their privacy policies.

4.3. AI Services

To operate the AI assistant, we may use external AI services that process your messages to generate responses.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. Storage

  • Data is stored on secure servers
  • Files are temporarily stored for processing
  • Chat history is stored for your convenience

5.2. Security

  • We use encryption for data transmission
  • We regularly update security measures
  • We limit access to personal data

6. Your Rights

6.1. Access and Control

  • View your data in your profile
  • Delete your account and all related data
  • Export your data

6.2. Advertising Preferences

  • Disable personalized ads in device settings
  • Learn what data Google AdMob collects
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6.3. File Management

  • Delete attached files
  • Clear chat history
  • Control access to gallery and microphone

7. App Permissions

7.1. Microphone

  • Purpose: Voice message input
  • Usage: Only when actively using voice input
  • Storage: Audio is not saved, only transcribed text

7.2. Gallery/Files

  • Purpose: Attaching documents and images
  • Usage: Only when files are selected by the user
  • Storage: Temporarily for AI assistant processing; files are not permanently stored

7.3. Internet

  • Purpose: Communication with servers for app functionality
  • Usage: Sending messages, uploading files, showing ads

8. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published in the app or in the app store listing. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.
